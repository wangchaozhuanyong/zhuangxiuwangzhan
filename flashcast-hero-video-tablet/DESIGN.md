## Style Prompt

Quiet luxury homepage film for FLASH CAST SDN. BHD. Tablet portrait must feel premium, organized, and readable without borrowing the phone crop too tightly or the desktop crop too widely. Use refined Malaysian renovation imagery, warm material tone, restrained text, and clean bilingual hierarchy.

## Colors

- Charcoal black: `#0D0C0A` for depth and text contrast.
- Warm white: `#FFF7EA` for headline text.
- Champagne gold: `#D1AD72` for brand accents and small labels.
- Walnut brown: `#6E4A30` for interior warmth.
- Stone greige: `#B7AA98` for quiet architectural balance.

## Typography

- Chinese: `Noto Sans SC` for readable Chinese slogan text.
- Labels and English: `Montserrat` for compact premium support text.

## Motion

- Slow push-in and subtle vertical drift only.
- Soft crossfades between scenes.
- Text enters with restrained opacity and vertical movement.
- Keep the middle of the frame open for homepage buttons.

## What NOT to Do

- No overcrowded bilingual blocks.
- No center text that competes with homepage CTAs.
- No aggressive zoom that makes source visuals soft.
- No glitter, neon, lens flare, or loud black-gold cliches.
